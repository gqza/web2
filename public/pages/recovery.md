# Recovery
*by Zavi*

> *"This won't be a sappy post, I promise"*

I had no real New Year's Resolution going into 2026, honestly. When I started this website, I was starting senior year, as well as a little job, and other responsibilities relating to family. I had multiple, conflicting intentions for this site;

- By November (site creation,) I attempted to create this website to vent and better display my artwork and struggles. Like a proof of progress thing. It was really minimalist.
> - I then realized that that seemed angsty and stopped posting altogether by December.
- By late January I decided to be a bit more positive with my blogposts, deciding to restart my journaling process.
> - I then had the grand realization[^1] that that idea was "gay" and stopped posting by March.

... and this cycle sort of continued.

If I really wanted to reach, I'd say that this was a byproduct of me fighting myself on a lot of things. This weird infighting, along with all the other things that life threw, caused me to overthink a lot of things. It caused me to deal with people that I *thought* existed. It told me to "use my mind" and yet it was fighting against me all of the time. I didn't fully realize that until it hurt the people I love the most. I didn't realize it until i had truth confused with perception.

That being said I am changing, very slowly. I've graduated, and no one is hiring, so I now have a complete and sincere obligation to be a fat[^2] unemployed chud. I am attempting to pause in real time. Because I think I have that now. Whether you believe that to be self-fellating or not is *totally* ***NOT*** my problem.
